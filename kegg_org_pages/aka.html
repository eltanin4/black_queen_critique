CommentChemolithoautotrophic betaproteobacterium.
Oxidizes thiosulfate by a poorly understood mechanism called the tetrathionate intermediate (S4I) pathway.
While a complete inorganic sulfur oxidation gene cluster, soxCDYZAXWB, is present in its genome, pathogenicity islands or genes associated with virulence, disease, cellular invasion, and/or intracellular resistance are completely absent.
Isolated from soil from a temperate orchard in Jammu and Kashmir, India.
